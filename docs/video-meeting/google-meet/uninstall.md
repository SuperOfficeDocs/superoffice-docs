# How to uninstall the Google video provider

Uninstalling the Google Meet video meeting service:

1. Sign in to your [Google account][1] and navigate to Security.

    ![imageqb9db.png][img1]

2. Click **Manage third-party access**.

    ![image767ll.png][img2]

3. Find the SuperOffice VideoMeetings app in the list, click to open it.

    ![imagebwcao.png][img3]

4. Click **Remove Access**.

    ![imageoicbe.png][img4]

5. Confirm remove access.

    ![imagef35lq.png][img5]
