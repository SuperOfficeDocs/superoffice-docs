# Enter phone calls

1. Do one of the following:

    * Choose **New** > **Phone Call** on the [top bar][2].
    * Click **Task** ( ![icon][img1] ) > **Make a phone call** in a contact card.

1. In the **Phone call** dialog, [specify the phone call details][1].
