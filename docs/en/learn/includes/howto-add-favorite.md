To add a record to favorites, click the star icon ![icon][img1] in the corner of the relevant card, tab or dialog. The star turns yellow ![icon][img2], and the record is instantly added to favorites. You do not have to click **Save**.

Now you can quickly access the record by clicking ![icon][img3] from the Navigator menu. The favorites are also available in the side panel.
