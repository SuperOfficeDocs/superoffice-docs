# First time use/log in

The Mobile CRM app is available for all users of SuperOffice CRM 9 or higher. It is distributed via the respective operating system's app store channels.

> [!NOTE]
> For older versions, install [Pocket CRM][5]. Check the [client requirements][4].

## Download the app

1. Access [Apple AppStore][6] or [Google Play][7], depending on your device.

    ![Mobile CRM download -app-screen][img6]

    Or, open a browser on your device and go to the [SuperOffice AppStore][8] to install the app.

1. Download and install the **SuperOffice Mobile CRM** app.

## Log in to Mobile CRM

1. Open the app on your device.
2. Enter your SuperOffice username and password.

![Mobile CRM: login -app-screen][img5]![Mobile CRM: log in -app-screen][img4]

> [!NOTE]
> Your sign-in method may depend on how your administrator has set up authentication (for example, Microsoft Entra ID).

## Log out

1. Tap <i class="ph ph-list" aria-label="Main menu"></i> the in the bottom center of the screen.
1. Select **Settings**.
1. Flick to the bottom and tap **Log out**.
1. Select **Yes** to confirm.
