# Save to SuperOffice

SuperOffice for Outlook lets you save email-related information directly to SuperOffice without leaving your inbox.

## In this section

* [Archive email to SuperOffice][1] – Archive an incoming email and its details to a contact, sale, or request in SuperOffice.
* [Save attachments to SuperOffice][2] – Save one or more email attachments directly to SuperOffice.
* [Send and archive][3] – Send a new email and automatically archive it to SuperOffice at the same time.
* [Email Archive Reminder][4] – Get reminded to archive emails you may have forgotten to save.
