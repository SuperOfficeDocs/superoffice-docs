# AI summaries

If your organization has AI Labs activated, SuperOffice for Teams can generate a summary of the conversation so you or a colleague can quickly understand the outcome without re-reading every message.

## Generate a summary

1. In the archive window, check **Generate Summary**.
2. The **Create** button changes to **Preview**.
3. Review the generated summary in the editable text area and adjust it if needed.
4. Click **Create** to save.

![The Preview screen showing an AI-generated summary in the editable text area][img1]

The summary is added to the **Description** field of the SuperOffice document. The full conversation is still stored in the attached .EML file.

## Current limitations

* Summaries are returned in neutral, professional business language as a single block of text.
* The tone and format of the summary cannot be customized. Other formats may be considered in a future release.

> [!NOTE]
> If **Generate Summary** is unavailable, AI Labs is not activated for your SuperOffice tenant. Contact your SuperOffice administrator.
