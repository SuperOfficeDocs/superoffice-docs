# Settings and maintenance 10.2.3 update

## CRMScript with time and date

Working with CRMScript and script triggered dialogs is complex and needs a lot of fine tuning. With support of more fields for Date, Time, DateTime, and Timespan, you have better possibilities to get wanted functionality.

Read more about the support in the [API][1] section.
