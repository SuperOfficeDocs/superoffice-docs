# Settings and maintenance 10.2.10 update

## Time zones and Daylight saving time

From time to time there is changes in the time zones and Daylight saving time. To get those updated you need to go to Settings and maintenance, select the **Option** navigator and then **Time zones** tab. Click the **Download new time zone updates** link, and the system will be updated in no time. You are all good to continue using time zones and the change for Daylight saving time is updated.

If you don't use time zones in your system, then you don't need to update this.
