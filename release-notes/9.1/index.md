# What’s new in SuperOffice 9.1

## Summary of highlights

[!include[G9 intro](../includes/G9-intro.md)]

SuperOffice 9.1 – is the first release of G9 and is the current version of the SuperOffice CRM platform.

The new version offers intuitive design combined with new ways to maximize and utilize your screen size. SuperOffice 9.1 also contains more options to organize and configure the way you view your customer information.

## Details of the new features

* 9.1 R01 New graphical user interface and the new side panel
* 9.1 R02 SuperOffice launches a new mobile CRM app
