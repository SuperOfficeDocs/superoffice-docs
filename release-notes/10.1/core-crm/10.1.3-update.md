# Core CRM 10.1.3 update

## Settings and maintenance link from Sales opens new tab

From the Sales module you have access to Settings and maintenance in the Main menu.

![Settings and maintenance link from Sales opens new tab -screenshot][img1]

When selecting the menu item you will open a new tab for Settings and maintenance and can continue to work in the Sales module.
