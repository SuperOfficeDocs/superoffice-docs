# Development products 10.1.2 update

## AI Services – In production

The pilot program for [AI Services available from 10.0.1][1] is ending on May 31st 2022. We will automatically cancel your SuperOffice AI pilot licenses by this date. From June 1st 2022, all services are billable with a fixed price per month per service.

* If you have been a pilot, you need to contact us to continue with AI Services.
* If you want to start using it, please contact us.

Head over to [the community pilot forums][2] to read about whom to contact and what to do if you want to use AI Services.
