# Mobile CRM 11.1.11 update

## Image background remover

A clean, neutral background makes **profile** and reference **images** look consistent and professional across your CRM. When adding or updating an image for a person, company, project, or document, you can take a photo directly in the app and remove distracting or noisy backgrounds in a single tap.

You'll find the option wherever you add or update an image in the app - for contacts, companies, projects, and documents alike.

Simply take or select a photo, then use the **remove background** action (<i class="ph ph-eraser" aria-hidden="true"></i>) to clean it up before saving. **Crop and rotate** options are also available on the same screen.
