# Mobile CRM 11.1.11 update

## Image background remover

When adding or updating a contact’s profile image, you can now take a photo directly in the app and instantly remove distracting or noisy backgrounds. This makes it easier than ever to create clean, professional‑looking profile images with a neutral background. This is perfect for keeping your CRM contact entries consistent and visually polished.

You will also have the same functionality available for companies, projects and documents that are images.
