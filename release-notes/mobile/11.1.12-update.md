# Mobile CRM 11.1.12 update

## Requests - Reply to a single message

You can reply to a specific message within a request in Mobile CRM, not just the request as a whole.

When replying, you can ensure the response is sent to the relevant contacts, including external contacts who are not registered in SuperOffice CRM. This makes it easier to keep conversations focused and communicate with the right people directly, even when working on the go.

![Request reply to a single message -app-screen][img1]

## Activate biometric login for Mobile CRM

Is it a struggle to remember all passwords and maybe have two-factor authentication? If yes, you can activate the biometric login for Mobile CRM, and it's one less hassle to your daily life.

When open up Mobile CRM you get a question: **Do you want to enable fingerprint login?** Just click yes (or no) and you are ready to go.
