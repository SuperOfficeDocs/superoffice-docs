# Core CRM and Sales 11.11 update

## User setup to configure e-mail and document management

*Available for new customers from 11.11, available for all customers from 11.13.*

As a new user (or a user with a new PC), there are a few things you should set up to ensure a smooth interaction between SuperOffice CRM, your e‑mail account, and your document handler.

Connecting your e‑mail account enables you to send e‑mails and automatically archive outgoing messages in SuperOffice, making all communication available to your colleagues. You will also be able to send meeting invitations to external recipients, receive invitations from others, and get responses when invitations are accepted or declined.

Setting up your document handler is equally important, as it provides an easy and efficient way to create, edit and open documents in SuperOffice.

![SuperOffice User setup - overview of first screen of wizard -screenshot][img1]

With the **User setup** found under your **Personal settings** menu,  a setup wizard will guide you through the process.

The second step is to connect your e‑mail account - simply click the **Connect** button and follow the steps to validate your login credentials.

![SuperOffice User setup - connect to your e-mail account -app-screen][img2]

The next step is to connect your document handler to SuperOffice. This is done by downloading WebTools, installing it, and then connecting it to the correct SuperOffice CRM tenant.

![SuperOffice User setup - download WebTools for handling documents -app-screen][img3]

> [!NOTE]
> We recommend installing [**SuperOffice for Outlook**][1] (or [**SuperOffice Gmail Link**][2])  to enable full e‑mail handling, including support for incoming messages from your mailbox.

Read more about [Connect email, document, and calendar][3] in Learn | Docs.
