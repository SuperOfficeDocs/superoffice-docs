# Core CRM and Sales 11.11 update

## User setup to configure e-mail and document management

As a new user (or a user with new pc) there are a couple of things you should do to make a good interaction with SuperOffice CRM and your e-mail account and your document handler.

The connection with your e-mail account will support outgoing e-mails and archive them in SuperOffice so all outgoing communication are available to your colleagues. You will also be able to send meeting invitations to externals, get meeting invitations from externals, and also the feedback of accepted or declined invitations.

The document handler is important to set up to get a good and easy system to create and edit documents in SuperOffice.

![SuperOffice User setup - overview of first screen of wizard -screenshot][img1]

With the **User setup** found under your **Personal settings** menu, you get a wizard on how to set this up seamlessly. Where the first step is to connect to your own e-mail account, just click the **Connect** button and follow the steps where you validate your login credentials.

The next step is connect your document handler to your SuperOffice, and that is done by downloading our WebTools, install it and then connect to the correct SuperOffice CRM tennant.
