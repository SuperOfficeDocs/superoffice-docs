# Settings and maintenance 11.11 update

## Subscription page styling - Change text Unsubscribe and Confirmations pages

In the Subscription page styling overview, you can also edit the text for several related pages from the Options tab, including the Unsubscribe, Confirmation saved, and Confirmation cancelled pages.

When you select the page you want to update, a preview automatically appears on the right side of the screen. This preview makes it easy to review your changes and see how they appear across the different languages you support.
