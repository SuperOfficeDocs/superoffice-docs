# Settings and maintenance 11.11 update

## Subscription page styling - fine tune unsubscribe and confirmation text

In the overview for Subscription page styling, you can also change the text for multiple pages in the Options tab, like the Unsubscribe, Confirmation saved and Confirmation cancelled pages.

Selecting the wanted page to change will give you automatically the preview on the right side of your screen. With that preview it's easy to see the changes for the different languages you have.
