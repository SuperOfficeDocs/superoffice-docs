# Pocket CRM 9.0.5 update

## The clickable buttons at the bottom are larger

There are multiple design changes to improve the usability of Pocket CRM

![Mobile -app-screen][img1]

Here are some of the key changes:

* The clickable buttons at the bottom of the screens are now larger, so it's easier to press them.

* New icons to make it easier to navigate through the different modules.

All these changes help to increase the usability of your mobile app.
