# SuperOffice Mobile CRM 10.1.4 update

## Mobile CRM – Dashboard support

The support of new functionality on **Dashboard** in the desktop client.

You will see the **Pinned** and **Favorites** dashboards as tabs, together with the search for dashboards.

![Dashboard support in mobile -screenshot][img1]
![Dashboard support in mobile -screenshot][img2]

You also have the possibility to add existing dashboards as your favorites by clicking on the star, and with easy access from the **Favorites** tab.
