# SuperOffice Marketing 10.0.4 update

## Mailing – Add video in content of the mailing

Sometimes, you want to show a video in the mailing and not just send a link.

1. Go to the mailing.

2. Add VIDEO from the Content section.

    ![Add VIDEO from the Content section -screenshot][img1]

3. Add a YouTube or Vimeo URL to automatically generate a preview image. The image will link to the provided URL.

    ![Add a YouTube or Vimeo URL -screenshot][img2]

**Settings for video content:**

* Play icon type
* Play icon color
* Play icon size
* Container padding (top, bottom, left, right)
* Responsive design with Hide on Desktop/Mobile (on/off)

> [!NOTE]
> The only video URLs supported are from YouTube or Vimeo.
