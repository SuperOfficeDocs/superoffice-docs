# Settings and maintenance 10.5.1 update

## SuperOffice Copilot - Easy to get started

AI Lab consist of a lot of different features and functions. Some of them are very useful in your daily work. To get started with SuperOffice AI Lab, you need to give your consent in Settings and maintenance.

Click the Feature Labs button and a new screen opens where you set the slider on for Pilot program: AI Lab and click Accept on the dialog. 

You are now good to go to start using [SuperOffice AI Lab][1] with all the different magic.
