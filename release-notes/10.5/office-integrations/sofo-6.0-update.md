# Office integrations 6.0 update

## SuperOffice for Outlook update – Action required

Microsoft has introduced enhanced security measures for accessing email and calendar data. To ensure continued use of the add-in, a Microsoft 365 Global Administrator must authorise the add-in and grant permissions (consent) on behalf of all users.

For detailed instructions, visit: SuperOffice for Outlook: [Authorisation guide][1].
